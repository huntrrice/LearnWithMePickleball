About Me

Hello! My name is Daniel Skrade and I am a pickleball fanatic. When teaching pickleball clinics my opening line is commonly "Playing pickleball is my favorite thing in the world, and teaching pickleball is my second." I have been around racket sports my whole life, starting tennis as early as I could hold a racket, had a ping pong table in the basement growing up, and started pickleball in 3rd Grade (2012).

I especially love pickleball for two reasons: The variability and strategy involved with every shot, and because there is constantly something new to learn. I am very thankful to be living in this current pickleball era where there aren't set shots, strategies, techniques, or paddles needed to win. While I will learn a lot throughout the process of making videos, the purpose of this channel is to help you learn. I sincerely hope you enjoy the content.
